Site Purpose

The business name is Southeast Idaho CO-OP

The domain will be "southeastidaho.com"

This is a community site for the people of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven to connect, share news, and promote their community. This site will have a page for each community, a home page, a weather page, and an image gallery page. The home page will contain a current weather story with corresponding images and an information section for the three towns. Each town site will contain weather conditions and other town information and content. The site will support responsive views so that users will have a clean experience on any device.
