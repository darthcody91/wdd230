Target Audience

The target audience is primarily directed at those in the communities of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven.

Personas

Golda June

Golda is a 80 year old widow who lives in Preston. Her grandkids and great-grandkids visit her often becuase she has a big yard for them to play in. She likes to know the weather, to know what the kids are going to be able to do. She has a hardtime with the internet and technology in general. The newspaper price keeps increasing, it doesn't get delivered everyday, and it's outdated. Her daughter is trying to teach her how to be comfortable with the internet, and wants to find a better source for news and weather.

Jeb Jingleheimer Schmidt

The brother of John Jacob, Jeb, is a potato farmer from Soda Springs. He spends his days tending to his precious potatoes, and loves to sell them locally becuase he is a big believer in small town business. He is having a hard time keeping up with getting ads into the newspaper and wants a better way of spreading word about his potatoes. He is in his 60's, and has experience with technology. Always gets the newest iPhone's so he can Facetime with the grandkids.

Scenarios

  • What's the weather supposed to be tomorrow?
  • Where can I tell people about my potatoes?
  • I'm free this weekend, wonder what there is to do...
  • I'm terrified of technology, I don't know where to find what I need.
  • I want to show my friends in Fish Haven and Soda Springs pictures of the kids.
