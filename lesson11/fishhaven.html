Soda Springs Spotlight

This week in Soda Springs Spotlight, we want to introduce Mr. Jeb Jinglehimershimt. The brother of John Jacob, Jeb, is a potato farmer from our beloved Soda Springs. He spends his days tending to his precious potatoes, and loves to sell them locally becuase he is a big believer in small town business.

He has been a "tater" farmer his whole life. And he wouldn't have it any other way. He knows the joy that potatoes bring to people's lives all over the world. He likes to call him the Santa Claus of food. His love of potatoes started at a young age, when his father was a banker in the big city. His father would bring home bags of chips, and french fries to share to the twenty children of the family. Jeb knew that he wanted to grow potatoes when he ate those fries. Though, he never understood what is so french about them...

This has been a tough year for Jeb and his farm. He is holding a special sale to sell as many bags of potatoes before the year is up.
