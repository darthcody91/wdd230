Friends of Fish Haven

This week in Friends of Fish Haven, we want to spotlight Ms. Beverly Fisher. Bev, as her friends call her, has lived her whole life in Fish Haven. She loves to fish and spend time with the grandkids. Fishing with the grandkids has become the best part of her life. She can teach them what her father taught her when she was their age. From things like what bait to use, where to fish and what even to dress like. They have been able to grow their relationships because of fishing. This last year, her grandson Frank convinced her to try ice fishing in the winter. She had never gone before, but thought that it would be fun if she could stay warm. She fell in love. She would rather ice fish, than regular fish. She says it's because she can spend more one-on-one time with the kdis while they patiently wait.

Bev loves cooking the fish. Preparing the fish is the hardest part she says. You have to be careful when trimming the skin off, and picking out the bones. She says that it has taken many years to get good preparing the fish, but a skill that she uses often and teaches her grandkids.
